ប្រើ Linux។

## Important Commands

- **Show files:** `ls`
- **Change directory:** `cd foldername`
- **Show current path:** `pwd`
- **Create folder:** `mkdir myfolder`
- **Remove file:** `rm filename`
- **Copy file:** `cp file1 file2`
- **Move file:** `mv file1 folder`
